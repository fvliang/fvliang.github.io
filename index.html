I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University. I am currently advised by Prof. Chen Tian and Zhibin Wang. Previously, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from The School of Future Technology in Harbin Institute of Technology in 2025.

Research

I am broadly interested in LLM systems. My current research focuses on Speculative Decoding for Large Language Models.
